NJCL Convention

July 24-29, 2021 on Zoom

We're so excited for NJCL Convention this year! Below, you'll find all the convention information we have for you at this time. Come back periodically for updates!

Keep in mind that you'll need to return a signature form after you've paid for Convention and the deadlines for Creative and Graphic Arts are again earlier this year, register early to ensure that you'll be able to participate. Additionally, a week before Convention, you'll receive a link via email to Sched, where you'll be able to select the events you wish to attend and locate their respective Zoom links.

Contact an officer, the Magistrae, or the listed emails on the linked documents if you have questions.
