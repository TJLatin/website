VJCL Convention

Nov. 14, 2021, 9 AM - 7 PM, Greater Richmond Convention Center

Nov. 15, 2021, 7 PM - 9 PM, Virtual

We're absolutely delighted that VJCL Convention is happening in person this year! The Domini Telae will be updating this website as we receive more information on the convention as the situation evolves, with a "Nota Bene" section for special announcements. Contact an officer or the Magistrae if you have questions.

Last updated: Oct. 11, 2021.

Nota Bene / TL;DR

  • BRING YOUR OWN CLIPBOARD AND PENCILS. This is a result of COVID protocols.
  • We will each be receiving a bag lunch and box dinner. GRCC Food Court will remain closed, as will Starbucks in the Marriott to students.
  • There will be no Sight Latin or Impromptu Art. That's Entertainment will be virtual.
  • Graphic Arts will be virtual, and each student is permited a maximum of 6 submissions.
  • Costume Contest will be virtual, while all other Creative Arts will remain in person.
  • Proof of vaccination or PCR test no earlier than Nov. 11 will be required to attend.
  • Whenever possible, students are required to wear surgical masks over cloth masks due to research pointing at their increased effectiveness.
